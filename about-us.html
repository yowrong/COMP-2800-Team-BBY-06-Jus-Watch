About Us

Miss movie nights with your friends? We at Jus'Watch do too! We are a team of developers with a strong passion for movies (good and bad), and want to use our skills to help you reconnect with your friends during these times. That's why we have come together to create an online Movie Club where you can nominate, vote on, and discuss everything movies with your friends!
